  • What is Deforestation?

    Deforestation is the intentional clearing of forested land. The most common ways of clearing trees are burning them(man-made wildfire that clear the entire forest) and slash-and-burn(vegetation on the land is cut and anything that remains is burned down). It is one of the biggest factors of climate change. Click here to learn more about deforestation and why it is done.

    Why Does it Matter?

    There is the obvious impact of deforestation: there are fewer trees on Earth. But that has a lot of consequences. It means that less carbon gets absorbed by those trees. It means releasing carbon that was stored in the trees. It means that habitats get destroyed and numerous species go extinct. It means losing the Earthâ€™s lungs.
